ABOUT ME

Hello design team folks at Doist,


Raised in a small town in the Dominican Republic. From an early age, I loved the idea of building things and helping to create products and services.


Nowadays, I am a product designer building and scaling stuff for my clients. I love what I do, and the contributions I have made to my clients earned me a respectable designer's profile. However, indeed "great just isn't good enough."; there is something fundamental missing when you work independently; the feeling of growth alongside others.

WHY DOIST?

I believe that as a Doist Product Designer I would have the opportunity to create real value for millions of people building the future. Besides, working in a company as forward-thinking and diverse as Doist would allow me to continue doing what I most like, namely facing new challenges on a regular basis and working out innovative solutions.


Finally, I am just a big fan of Doist and it’s different products. I like its history (it's being born with humble beginnings), and I share it’s core principle.
